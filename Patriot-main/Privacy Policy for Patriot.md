# Privacy Policy for Patriot

**Last updated: November 27, 2025**

## Overview
Patriot is a 2D action-shooter game that respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
Patriot does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data. The game:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not collect analytics or usage data
- Does not use advertising SDKs
- Does not require internet connectivity
- Runs entirely offline on your device

## Permissions
The app only requests permissions necessary for core gameplay functionality.

## Children's Privacy
Patriot does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, contact: patriot.game.support@gmail.com

## Changes
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
